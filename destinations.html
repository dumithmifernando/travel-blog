A small town nestled in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, Ella offers stunning views, lush tea plantations, and a cool climate. It's perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Activities

  • Hike to Ella Rock for panoramic views
  • Visit the scenic Nine Arches Bridge
  • Explore Ravana Falls
  • Tour local tea plantations and factories

Known as the "Lion Rock," Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site. It combines history, archaeology, and breathtaking landscapes.

Activities

  • Climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress and explore its frescoes and gardens
  • Discover the ancient city ruins and water gardens
  • Explore Minneriya National Park for elephant safaris nearby
  • Visit the nearby Pidurangala Rock for an alternative viewpoint

A historic coastal city famous for its well-preserved Dutch Fort, Galle combines colonial charm with beautiful beaches.

Activities

  • Walk along the Galle Fort walls
  • Explore colonial architecture, museums, and boutique shops
  • Relax or surf at nearby beaches like Unawatuna
  • Enjoy fresh seafood at local restaurants
